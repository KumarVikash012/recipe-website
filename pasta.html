Indian Style Masala Pasta

⏱ 30 Minutes 👤 Serves 3 🌍 Indian Fusion Easy

Quick Info

Prep10 min
Cook20 min
DifficultyEasy
CategoryVeg
Serves3

Ingredients

  • 250g pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 capsicum, sliced
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander for garnish

Quick Overview

A street-style Indian pasta — spicy, buttery, slightly tangy. Think pasta meets masala maggi energy, but upgraded.

Instructions

  1. Boil Pasta

    Cook pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and keep aside.

  2. Make Base

    Heat butter, sauté onion till soft. Add ginger-garlic paste.

  3. Add Masala

    Add tomato puree, chilli powder, turmeric, salt. Cook till thick.

  4. Mix Veggies

    Add capsicum and cook for 2–3 minutes.

  5. Combine

    Add pasta + ketchup + garam masala. Toss well.

  6. Serve

    Garnish with coriander and serve hot.

Tip

Add cheese on top if you want it creamy. Skip if you want it more street-style.
