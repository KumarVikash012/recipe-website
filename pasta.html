Masala Pasta Street Style

⏱ 30 Minutes 👤 Serves 3 🌍 Indian Fusion Easy

Quick Info

Prep Time 10 minutes
Cook Time 20 minutes
Difficulty Easy
Cuisine Indian Fusion
Category Vegetarian
Serves 3 persons

Ingredients

  • 250g pasta
  • 2 tbsp oil or butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 capsicum, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 2 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander for garnish
Indian Street Favorite

Simple, Spicy, Comforting

Masala pasta is a quick Indo-fusion dish with a spicy tomato base, soft vegetables, and a bold street-style taste.

It is fast, filling, and perfect for when you want something familiar with a little extra kick.

Instructions

Step-by-Step Method

  1. Boil the Pasta

    Cook pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. Make the Base

    Heat oil or butter, then sauté onion, capsicum, and ginger-garlic paste.

  3. Add Masala

    Mix in tomato puree, chilli powder, turmeric, garam masala, salt, and ketchup.

  4. Cook the Sauce

    Let the masala cook until thick and well combined.

  5. Combine

    Add the pasta and toss well so everything gets coated.

  6. Serve

    Garnish with coriander and serve hot.

Chef's Tip

Add a little extra butter at the end for a richer street-style finish.
