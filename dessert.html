Classic Rasmalai

⏱ 45 mins 👤 Serves 4 🌍 Indian Medium

Quick Info

Prep20 min
Cook25 min
DifficultyMedium

Ingredients

  • 1 liter milk
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 cups milk (for syrup)
  • 4-5 cardamom pods
  • Few saffron strands
  • Chopped pistachios

Simple Rasmalai

Soft paneer balls soaked in sweet saffron milk, served chilled as a rich Indian dessert.

Steps

  1. Make Paneer

    Boil milk, add lemon juice, and strain to get paneer.

  2. Shape Balls

    Knead paneer and form small smooth balls.

  3. Cook Balls

    Boil in sugar water for 10 minutes until soft.

  4. Prepare Milk

    Boil milk with sugar, cardamom, and saffron.

  5. Combine & Chill

    Add balls to milk, garnish, and chill before serving.

Chef Tip

Chill well before serving for best taste and texture.
