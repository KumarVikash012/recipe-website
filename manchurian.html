Veg Manchurian Street Style

⏱ 40 mins 👤 Serves 4 🌍 Indo-Chinese Medium

Quick Info

Prep 20 mins
Cook 20 mins
Cuisine Indo-Chinese
Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed veggies (cabbage, carrot, capsicum)
  • 3 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp maida
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chilli sauce
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • Spring onions
  • Salt & pepper
Indo-Chinese Classic

Quick & Fiery

Crispy veg balls tossed in a spicy, glossy sauce. Fast, bold, and best eaten hot.

Instructions

Steps

  1. Mix

    Combine veggies, cornflour, maida, salt. Form tight balls.

  2. Fry

    Deep fry till golden and crisp. Set aside.

  3. Sauce

    Stir-fry garlic. Add soy, chilli sauce, ketchup, little water.

  4. Thicken

    Add cornflour slurry. Cook till glossy.

  5. Toss

    Add balls, coat well. Cook 1 min.

  6. Serve

    Top with spring onions. Serve hot.

Chef's Tip

Double fry for extra crisp texture.
