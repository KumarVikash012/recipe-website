Classic Butter Chicken

⏱ 40 mins 👤 Serves 2 🌍 Indian Medium

Quick Info

Prep15 min
Cook25 min
DifficultyMedium

Ingredients

  • 400g chicken
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1/2 cup yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt

Simple Butter Chicken

A creamy tomato-based Indian curry made with tender chicken and mild spices.

Steps

  1. Marinate

    Mix chicken with yoghurt, salt, and spices. Rest 30 mins.

  2. Cook Chicken

    Cook chicken in a pan until lightly browned.

  3. Prepare Gravy

    Heat butter, add tomato puree and cook 5–10 mins.

  4. Combine

    Add chicken and cook together for 10 minutes.

  5. Finish

    Add cream, mix well, and serve hot.

Chef Tip

Cook on low heat after adding cream for best flavor.
