Royal Awadhi Biryani

⏱ 2 Hours 👤 Serves 6 🌍 Indian Hard

Quick Info

Prep Time 45 minutes
Cook Time 1 hr 15 mins
Marinating 4–8 hours
Difficulty Hard
Cuisine Indian (Awadhi)
Serves 6 persons

Ingredients

  • 500g lamb pieces
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup yoghurt
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 2 tsp biryani masala
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 3 tbsp ghee
  • Salt to taste
Classic Indian Dish

Simple Biryani

A flavorful rice dish made with spiced lamb and aromatic basmati rice, cooked together for a rich and satisfying meal.

Instructions

Step-by-Step Method

  1. Marinate the Lamb

    Mix lamb with yoghurt, spices, and salt. Rest for 30–60 minutes.

  2. Fry Onions

    Cook sliced onions in ghee until golden brown.

  3. Cook the Lamb

    Add marinated lamb and cook until mostly done.

  4. Boil Rice

    Cook rice until 70% done, then drain.

  5. Layer & Cook

    Layer rice over lamb, cover, and cook on low heat for 20 minutes.

Chef's Tip

Cook on low heat for best flavor and fluffy rice.
